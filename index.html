SUNDIAL

A narrative-driven game about memory, truth, and the cost of rewriting the world.

About the Game

In a world fractured by war and secrecy, Maria Araya searches for her missing father— a man erased from history by the mysterious Project Sundial. Set in a reimagined, post-war Slovakia, SUNDIAL is a choice-driven narrative game where every decision shapes the story. As alliances shift and enemies blur, will you uncover the truth, or rewrite it?

Biography

Maria Araya is a former special forces operative who left military service after discovering disturbing truths about her father's disappearance. Skilled in hand-to-hand combat and tactical operations, she now uses her skills to uncover secrets the powerful would rather keep buried.

Her journey takes her through the fractured remains of post-war Slovakia, where she must navigate complex political alliances while confronting her own fragmented memories of the war.

Key Relationships: Daughter of Dr. Emil Araya (missing), Former comrade of Kazi, Reluctant ally of Lenka

Biography

Lenka operates the most extensive information network in the Dead Zone, trading secrets for favors and survival. With a sharp wit and sharper tongue, she navigates the dangerous underworld of data trafficking with calculated precision.

Her past is deliberately obscured—whether by her own design or external forces remains unclear. What is certain is that she knows more about Project Sundial than she lets on, and her price for that information may be more than Maria is willing to pay.

Key Relationships: Information source for all factions, Business partner to the Black Market, Knew Dr. Araya before his disappearance

Biography

Once a decorated officer, Kazi now leads a rogue faction fighting against both the government and corporate forces controlling post-war Slovakia. His methods are controversial, but his conviction is unwavering.

Haunted by his actions during the war, Kazi sees Project Sundial as both a threat and potential weapon. His relationship with Maria is complicated by their shared past—they were once comrades, but the truth about her father's disappearance may make them enemies.

Key Relationships: Former comrade of Maria, Enemy of the current regime, Suspected by Lenka

Story Acts

Act 1: SHADOWS OF WAR

Maria's search begins in a fragmented Slovakia controlled by rival factions. As she follows rumors of her father's survival, she's pulled into a struggle between nationalism, rebellion, and corporate manipulation. Trust is a weapon. History is rewritten.

Act 2: THREADS OF THE PAST

Leads point to a blacksite known as the Dead Zone—and Project Sundial, a memory-manipulation experiment that rewrote her father. Maria must choose her companion, confront betrayals, and walk into the unknown, hunted by the man who replaced her father's legacy.

Act 3: RESET THE WORLD

At the heart of the Sundial facility, Maria discovers a machine that rewrites identity. The final decision: activate it to rewrite the past… or destroy it and live with the scars. Her choices will shape the world's future—or erase it.
